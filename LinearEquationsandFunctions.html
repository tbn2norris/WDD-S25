Linear Equations and Functions

What is a Linear Equation?

A linear equation is an equation of the form ax + b = c, where a, b, and c are constants, and x is the variable.

What is a Linear Function?

A linear function is a function that can be expressed in the form f(x) = mx + b, where m is the slope and b is the y-intercept.

Examples

Example of a linear equation: 3x + 4 = 10

Example of a linear function: f(x) = 2x + 1

Practice Problems

  • Solve the linear equation: 2x + 3 = 11
  • Find the slope and y-intercept of the function: f(x) = 5x - 2
